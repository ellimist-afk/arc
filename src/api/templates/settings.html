How it works

The bot monitors silence periods and automatically generates responses after the threshold time passes. Choose the setting that matches your streaming style and content.

💬 Active (2m)
High engagement mode. Perfect for interactive gameplay, tutorials, or energetic streams where constant chatter keeps viewers engaged.
⚖️ Default (5m)
Balanced approach. Ideal for most content types, allowing natural conversation flow while preventing awkward long silences.
☕ Quiet (10m)
Relaxed setting. Great for chill streams, creative work, or when you need longer focus periods without interruption.
🌙 Minimal (15m)
Ultra-low intervention. Perfect for focused work, study sessions, or ambient streams where silence is intentional and comfortable.
